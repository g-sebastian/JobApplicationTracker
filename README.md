My current fixed mindset is that I believe I am not yet skilled enough in Computer Science to apply for internships or working student positions. 
I often feel that I lack practical experience and therefore hesitate to take action.

My challenge over the next six weeks is to actively build confidence in my abilities by developing one to two practical programming projects and applying to real companies.

In week 1, I will define a realistic project idea and choose the technologies.
In weeks 2–4, I will focus on implementing at least one complete project and documenting my progress on GitHub.
In week 5, I will finalize the project, improve my CV, and prepare application materials.
In week 6, I will apply to at least five companies and reflect on the feedback and my learning process.

Afterwards I will reflect on entire process and evaluate how my perception of my own skills has changed. My Goal is to move from self doubt to action and gain a more realistic understanding of my abilities through practical experience.
